What is Pregnancy Helper App?

Pregnancy Helper App is develop by two student of Western Mindanao State University, naming Viktor Greg Lim and John Mchales Buenaventura. The purpose of this app is to help guide specifically pregnant women and the spouse in there most struggle times together, starting to build a family.
