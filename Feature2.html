Marlin


Eating marlin during pregnancy is generally considered risky because marlin is a type of large predatory fish that can accumulate high levels of mercury in its tissues. Mercury is a toxic metal , and exposure to high levels during pregnancy can harm the developing fetus

Alcohol


Drinking alcohol during pregnancy is harmful because it can have serious negative effects on the developing fetus. Alcohol passes through the placenta and enters the baby’s bloodstream, which can affect the baby’s growth and development

Oysters


Risk of Foodborne Illness: Raw or undercooked oysters can harbor harmful bacteria, viruses, and parasites, such as Vibrio bacteria and norovirus, which can cause severe foodborne illnesses. .
