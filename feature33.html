Is taking medicine bad?

Not all medicines are bad during pregnancy. Some are necessary for the health of the mother and baby,
while others can pose risks, but, Some medications can harm fetal development, leading to complications or
birth defects. It's essential to consult with a healthcare provider about which medicines are safe.

Why is it important to take medicine when pregnant?

Certain conditions, like high blood pressure or diabetes, may require medication to manage health risks.
Ensuring the mother's health is crucialfor the baby's well-being, as untreated conditions can lead to more significant issues.
